Shrimp linguini is a classic pasta dish that pairs juicy shrimp with linguini in a flavorful sauce. It's similar to shrimp scampi, but shrimp scampi doesn't include pasta and is usually served with crusty bread. Shrimp linguini is a great dish to make for a special occasion or a romantic dinner. It's quick and easy to make. You can customize the dish by adding your favorite vegetables or herbs.
