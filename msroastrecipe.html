Mississippi Pot Roast Recipe

Serves 6–8 • Prep Time: 5 Minutes • Cook Time: 8+ Hours

Ingredients

  • 2–3 lbs chuck roast
  • 1–2 packets ranch dressing mix
  • 1 packet au jus gravy mix
  • 6 tbsp salted butter
  • 1/3 cup pepperoncini juice
  • 6–8 pepperoncini peppers

Instructions

  1. Place chuck roast into the crock pot.
  2. Sprinkle ranch and au jus mix evenly over roast.
  3. Add butter, peppers, and pepperoncini juice.
  4. Cook on LOW for 8+ hours.
  5. Taste juices around the 6-hour mark and adjust as desired.
  6. Shred roast and mix into cooking juices.
  7. Serve over noodles, rice, mashed potatoes, or toasted rolls.
💡 Tip: Add extra pepperoncini juice for a bolder tangy flavor.
