🍤 Shrimp Linguini

A bright, garlicky shrimp pasta tossed in butter, fresh herbs, and lemon — simple ingredients that make a beautiful, restaurant-quality meal at home.

Shrimp Linguini Recipe

Serves 4 • Prep Time: 10 minutes • Cook Time: 15 minutes

Ingredients

  • 8 oz linguine
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 8 tablespoons salted butter (1 stick)
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon red pepper flakes
  • 1 ¼ lb large shrimp
  • Salt, to taste
  • Pepper, to taste
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 4 cups baby spinach
  • ¼ cup parmesan cheese, grated
  • 2 tablespoons fresh parsley, chopped
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice

Instructions

  1. Boil a large pot of water, add pasta, and cook until al dente. Stir often.
  2. Drain pasta and set aside.
  3. In the same pot, heat olive oil and 2 tablespoons of butter. Add garlic and red pepper flakes; cook until fragrant.
  4. Add shrimp, salt, and pepper. Stir until shrimp start to turn pink but are not fully cooked.
  5. Add oregano and spinach; cook until the spinach wilts.
  6. Return pasta to the pot. Add remaining butter, parmesan, and parsley. Stir until butter melts and everything is well combined.
  7. When shrimp are fully cooked, stir in lemon juice and remove from heat.
  8. Serve hot and enjoy!

💡 Tip: Add extra lemon juice or parmesan for even more flavor.
